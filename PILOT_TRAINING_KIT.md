### 1. Order Buffer & SLA
- **Golden Rule:** You have **60 seconds** to click "Accept" once the loud ringing starts.
- **Escalation:** If ignored, a secondary "Urgent" alert will play, and the Admin will be notified of the latency.

### 2. Inventory Management
- During a rush, if a product (e.g., Mother Dairy Milk) runs out, go to **Live Inventory** and toggle it to **OFF**.
- This immediately removes the item from the Customer app to prevent cancellations.

### 3. Low Stock Alerts
- Keep an eye on the **Orange Warning** labels in your inventory. These appear when stock is below your safety threshold.
