# Sweeftcom Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 22, 2026

Sweeftcom ("we," "us," or "our") is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use your data.

## 1. Information Collection
*   **Identity Data:** Name, Phone Number, Email.
*   **Location Data:** Precise GPS coordinates (Rider app requires background access).
*   **Transaction Data:** Details of orders placed and payments made (handled via secure PCI-DSS compliant gateways).

## 2. Use of Background Location
The Rider App collects background location data to:
*   Provide real-time order tracking to customers.
*   Automate "At Store" status updates (Geofencing).
*   Monitor delivery safety and efficiency.

## 3. Data Sharing
We share necessary information with:
*   **Vendors:** To prepare your order.
*   **Riders:** To deliver to your address.
*   **Govt Authorities:** When required by law (e.g., GST filings).

## 4. Data Retention
We retain your data as long as your account is active. Financial records are retained for the statutory period required under Indian Tax Law.

## 5. Your Rights
You may access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data directly through the Sweeftcom application settings.

## 6. Contact Us
For any privacy-related queries, please contact: `privacy@sweeftcom.com`.
